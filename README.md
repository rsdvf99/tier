## What the Network Actually Looks Like Under the Hood

This isn't a company renting rack space from a third party and renting it back to you. The infrastructure details:

- **Cisco® ASR routers and Nexus™ switches** throughout
- **Multi-homed bandwidth** — no single upstream dependency
- **N+1 or 2N redundancy** at every datacenter location
- **Tier 3 certified facilities** — redundant power, cooling, physical security
- **Wholly owned hardware** — they built it, they maintain it

Multi-homed bandwidth matters more than most people realize. If your host's upstream provider has an issue, single-homed networks go dark. Multi-homed networks reroute. That's the difference between "brief degradation" and "everything is down."
